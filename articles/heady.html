Languages & Libraries

Background

For my end-of-year project, one of my co-worker and I decided to realize a request from a medical institute which to care of children affected by various diseases and disabilities, including the Rett syndrome.

Rett syndrome is a genetic brain disease that develops in very young children, mainly girls, and causes mental disabilities and severe motor impairments.
Symptoms include problems with language, coordination, and repetitive movements.
There is no known cure for Rett syndrome.

The main difficulty encountered by the medical staff with a patient affected by Rett syndrome is communication, due to the malformations and brain damages caused by the disease.

So to assist the personnel, the director of the establishment proposed us to realize a communication interface for the children.

Description

The project was seperated into two parts but I'm only going to talk about mine : the realisation of the interface.

The purpose of the interface is to help the children to express their feelings and requests, and the whole thing in a simple way enough to be understood by them. To do this, the interface takes all the screen and is divided into three sections.

Toolbar

It contains the following tab :

  • Enregistrement : save the coordinates of the cursor motion to make statistics
  • Menu 1, 2, 3 : switch between the different selections of expressions
  • Quitter : stop and quit the interface

Answer Zone

It inculdes two big buttons : one in red to say "NO" and the other in green to say "YES".

Expressions Zone

It composed of four big buttons which desbribe an expression like hunger, pain or sleep. Those expressions sets can't be changed by using the menu tabs.

When pressed, the buttons play an audio file defined according to the child.
